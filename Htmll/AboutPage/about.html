Our Story

Balloons by Twiin was founded in August 2022 by lifelong friends Shiela Marie Lara-Hisugan and Ac Montinola. With a passion for DIY balloon styling and a supportive family, they capitalized on the potential by starting the business. Specializing in party supplies, Balloons by Twiin targets young individuals celebrating birthdays and other special occasions.

Our goal

The main goal of their business, owned by Ms. Sheila Marie Lara-Hisugan and Ms. AC Montinola, is to provide high-quality balloon products that cater to various events and celebrations.
